Wamono: The Art of Japanese Rare Groove

Join us for an evening where timeless Japanese aesthetics converge with the art of vinyl curation. Set against the storied backdrop of Kamehachi in Old Town Chicago, this capsule event series offers a refined exploration of Japan’s cultural treasures, including a themed-gift bag.

An Immersive Experience
Under the masterful direction of DJ Van Paugam, prepare to journey through an exclusive collection of Japanese vinyl records spanning the golden years of 1975–1985. Expect rare grooves ranging from the shimmering city pop to the soulful strains of Showa kayo—each record chosen for its historical resonance and sonic excellence.
